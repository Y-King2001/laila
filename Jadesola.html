Jadesola

Not your will but fate
Not my will but your decision
Our small world is being introduced into extinction
The walls of our world went crumbling
Faster than even the speed of light
Once envied, now mocked at
Is it wrong to want to stay by each other's side forever?
What went wrong with our song of forever?
Now, Tuesdays are sad days
And Mondays now mourn days
The days breaks without your care
Our everything now nothing

If I could go back in time, I'd capture every moment
Maybe it could be fixed
Maybe I'd guard my heart better
All I had in my head was the SEVEN LETTER WORD "FOREVER"
Am unexpected end
No prior talks, no prior issues, no prior disagreement
Just a blunt end
"Everything has an end"
What a way to consider each other?
How do I say I miss you and what we had
Sisters not haters now people with blisters
Even fixes could not get this done
So now, we keep distance

A sunny Afternoon
Beautiful day ahead
Thought it was just about to be fun
From a thought, came a decision
From a decision, came a text
Just a text ruins the day
And now, we're back to bring strangers
